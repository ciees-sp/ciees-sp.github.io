A piece of writing, rewritten at six character budgets. Not summarized. Rewritten. Each version is complete, stands alone, does the job. The question is where the meaning changes. Not where it gets shorter. Where it becomes a different piece of writing.

Where does it stop being the same piece of writing?
Instance 15. The session's work was a social media post about women in leadership at CIEES. The original copy said "the women driving CIEES forward" and a director objected because the frame overclaimed. The fix was changing the headline, not the photo. Same faces, different caption, different post. At 500 characters the post made a claim about strategic direction. At 40 it celebrated a structural fact. The meaning didn't get shorter. It became a different claim. That's the question this piece asks.
