CPST Learner Personas

Marcus Rivera
Associate degree in human services
Behavioral Health Technician (BHT)
"What do I do, and how do I know I'm doing it right?"
About Marcus
Came to behavioral health after a family member's experience with mental health services. Engages quickly with new material when it's tied to a concrete situation. Learns best when clinical concepts are introduced in plain language before the formal terminology.
On the job, Marcus:
  • Reinforces skills between sessions using established scripts
  • Encourages clients to practice what the QMHP introduced
  • Documents observations and reports to the team
  • Screens, coordinates care, orients new clients
Outside Marcus's scope:
  • Clinical judgment or decision-making
  • Determining whether symptoms meet diagnostic criteria
  • Teaching new clinical content without an established script
  • Interpreting why a treatment plan includes what it includes
Adrienne Cole
Bachelor's degree in business administration
Qualified Mental Health Professional (QMHP)
"What do I need to know to walk in prepared?"
About Adrienne
Career-changed into behavioral health from retail management. Completed her QMHP coursework and supervision hours and has been registered for about two years. Comfortable with clients but still building confidence with clinical concepts. Learns well when content is grounded in what she'll actually do on the job.
On the job, Adrienne:
  • Teaches psychoeducation and coping skills directly to clients
  • Implements interventions assigned on the treatment plan
  • Introduces treatment rationale in plain language
  • Gathers histories, initiates crisis de-escalation
  • Covers BHT tasks (screening, care coordination, new client orientation) on teams without a BHT
  • Documents and coordinates with the team
Outside Adrienne's scope:
  • Diagnosis or differential diagnosis
  • Clinical assessment and formulation
  • Selecting which intervention to use
  • Modifying the treatment plan
  • Providing psychotherapy

Their supervisor: Lydia Chen, LPC, LMHP — assesses, writes the treatment plan, selects interventions, and supervises both Marcus and Adrienne weekly.
