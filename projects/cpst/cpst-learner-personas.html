CPST Learner Personas

Who is this training for?

Marcus Rivera
Behavioral Health Technician (BHT)
"Just tell me what to do and why it matters. I'll figure out the rest in the room."
Education: Associate degree in human services
Started in the field after a family member's experience with mental health services. Practical learner. Picks things up fast when he can connect them to something real.
Reads at roughly a 10th-grade level. Clinical terminology slows him down, but he'll engage with it if it's explained in plain language first.
On the job, Marcus:
  • Reinforces skills between sessions using established scripts
  • Encourages clients to practice what the QMHP introduced
  • Documents observations and reports to the team
  • Screens, coordinates care, orients new clients
Not Marcus's role:
  • Diagnosing or selecting interventions
  • Teaching new clinical content to clients
  • Designing or modifying treatment plans
  • Applying theoretical frameworks
Adrienne Cole
Qualified Mental Health Professional (QMHP)
"I want to understand why the plan says what it says, even if I'm not the one who wrote it."
Education: Bachelor's degree in business administration
Career-changed into behavioral health after working in retail management. Two years of experience. Comfortable with clients but still building confidence with clinical concepts.
Can follow clinical material when it's grounded in what she'll actually do. Gets bogged down when content assumes prerequisite knowledge from a clinical degree she doesn't have.
On the job, Adrienne:
  • Teaches psychoeducation and coping skills directly to clients
  • Implements interventions from the LMHP's treatment plan
  • Introduces treatment rationale in plain language
  • Gathers histories, initiates crisis de-escalation
  • Documents and coordinates with the team
Not Adrienne's role:
  • Diagnosing or conducting clinical assessments
  • Designing treatment plans or selecting interventions
  • Providing psychotherapy
  • Supervising other providers

Their supervisor: Lydia Chen, LPC, LMHP — assesses, writes the treatment plan, selects interventions, and supervises both Marcus and Adrienne weekly. Lydia has the clinical background to engage with these concepts at full depth. This training is for Marcus and Adrienne.

If Marcus can't follow it, it needs distillation. If Adrienne doesn't use it on the job, it's not in the module.
