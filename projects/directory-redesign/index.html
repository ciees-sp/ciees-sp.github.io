Accessible "Read More" Links

Given our need for WCAG AA compliance, the "Read more" links on this page will need screen reader text added so that each button announces the person's name (e.g., "Read more about [Name]" rather than identical "Read more" links). The theme already defines .screen-reader-text in CSS. The person's name is stored in the ACF field staff_directory_name — use get_field() to populate the hidden span automatically. Proposed pattern:

Read more<span class="screen-reader-text"> about <?php echo get_field('staff_directory_name'); ?></span>

Same idea for the toggled state:

Show less<span class="screen-reader-text"> about <?php echo get_field('staff_directory_name'); ?></span>

About CIEES

☗ CIEES-About CIEES
CIEES brings educators, youth and families, community members, public sector leaders, and policymakers together to create education systems innovation.

With powerful partners and immensely talented staff, the Center for Implementation and Evaluation of Education Systems (CIEES) is uniquely situated to create meaningful changes in the outcomes of students and adults with disabilities or marginalizing conditions. Focus areas include teacher preparation, systemwide leadership and evaluation, training and technical assistance, recruitment and retention, and engaging adult learners. CIEES believes that education is the platform for social change — change that ensures equitable opportunities for all individuals.
