Vision
We envision a world in which every individual has access to education that is meaningful, productive, and maximizes their potential for success.

Goals

CIEES will fulfill its vision and mission by

Assisting school divisions and community agencies in training and supporting education personnel for high-need teaching areas, particularly special education
Evaluating school division and community-based educational initiatives, programs, and services
Creating a trajectory of research initiatives to improve outcomes in educational settings
