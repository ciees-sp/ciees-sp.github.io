Notes

Accessible "Read More" Links — Required

Every "Read more" / "Show less" button must include a .screen-reader-text span containing the project title. Without it, screen readers announce nine identical "Read more" links with no context. The WordPress theme already defines .screen-reader-text in CSS — use that class.

Pattern:

Read more<span class="screen-reader-text"> about <?php echo get_the_title(); ?></span>

When the button toggles to "Show less," the hidden span must persist:

Show less<span class="screen-reader-text"> about <?php echo get_the_title(); ?></span>

The hidden text auto-populates from the portfolio item's title field via get_the_title() in the ACF template — no manual entry per card.

Virginia Department of Behavioral Health and Developmental Services

Module Series

Case Management for Substance Use Disorders

A 5-module series for case managers working with individuals and families affected by substance use disorders. Includes videos, case studies, and quizzes. Developed in partnership with the Virginia Department of Behavioral Health and Developmental Services (DBHDS), this 5-module series is designed for case managers who work with individuals and families affected by substance use disorders (SUDs). Each module includes a video, case study, and quiz and takes approximately one hour to complete. Topics include an introduction to case management, the continuum of care, engagement and retention strategies, and considerations for special populations. Certificates of completion are awarded upon passing the quiz with a score of 80% or higher.
Module Series

REVIVE! Opioid Overdose Awareness

Training modules on opioid risk awareness and the critical role of naloxone in overdose situations, for healthcare workers and community partners. The REVIVE! modules focus on educating healthcare workers and community partners about the risks associated with opioid use and the critical role of naloxone in overdose situations. The modules cover key requirements for dispensing naloxone and provide essential guidance for individuals who administer it in emergency settings.
Module Series

Working with Adults with IDD & Substance Use Disorders

Modules for community services board clinicians providing outpatient treatment to adults with intellectual or developmental disabilities and co-occurring SUDs. Developed in partnership with the Virginia Department of Behavioral Health and Developmental Services (DBHDS), this 6-module series is designed for community services board (CSB) clinicians who provide outpatient treatment to adults with intellectual or developmental disabilities (IDD) and co-occurring substance use disorders (SUDs). Each module includes a video, case study, and quiz and takes approximately one hour to complete. Topics include an overview of IDD, unique treatment considerations, trauma-informed care approaches, and strategies for building effective support systems. Certificates of completion are awarded upon passing the quiz with a score of 80% or higher.

Virginia Department of Education

Module Series

Office of Dispute Resolution Learning Library

Two resource areas for special education professionals: practical guidance videos and an interactive timeline of the special education due process system. Developed in partnership with VDOE's Office of Dispute Resolution and Administrative Services (ODRAS), this library offers two resource areas for special education professionals: (1) practical guidance videos addressing common questions about dispute resolution, and (2) an interactive timeline of the special education due process system.
Module Series

Regulations Governing the Use of Restraint & Seclusion

A training series on Virginia's restraint and seclusion regulations for school division staff, with videos, knowledge checks, and certificates of completion. Developed in partnership with the Virginia Department of Education (VDOE), this training series is designed for school division staff responsible for understanding and implementing Virginia's regulations on the use of restraint and seclusion. Each module includes a video, knowledge check, and certificate of completion.
Module Series

Virtual Learning Support Toolkit

Eleven asynchronous modules giving school leaders and educators research-based tools for effective virtual and blended instruction. Developed in partnership with VDOE's Office of STEM and Innovation, this series of 11 asynchronous modules gives school leaders and educators research-based tools for effective virtual and blended instruction. Topics span supporting positive student behavior online, planning and structuring virtual lessons, strategies for student engagement and participation, and leveraging technology to personalize learning.
Resource Hub Learning Platform

Career & Learning Center for School Mental Health Professionals

A one-stop professional development and resource collection for school counselors, psychologists, social workers, nurses, and administrators. In partnership with CIEES, the VDOE Office of Student Services (OSS) offers a one-stop professional development and resource collection for specialized instructional support personnel such as school counselors, school psychologists, school social workers, school nurses, and administrators.
Learning Platform

Creating Capacity for Change Collaborative

A learning hub supporting the implementation of multi-tiered systems of supports across Virginia's school divisions, with video series, quizzes, and certificates. A learning hub developed in collaboration with the Virginia Department of Education to support the implementation of a multi-tiered system of supports across Virginia's school divisions. Educators and division leaders will find multiple video series with quizzes and certificates of completion covering topics in positive behavior, academic support systems, and data-driven decision making.
