Statement of Work Supplemental Information

DBHDS State Opioid Response Training Architecture

Design of a statewide training system for Virginia's State Opioid Response workforce across the prevention, treatment, and recovery continuum.

Development Plan
LMS Prototype

Activities and Deliverables

Assessing provider training needs and facilitating the development of the scope and sequence for a statewide online training curriculum.

Year 1

Assessment of Training Needs (CIEES)

Surveys and focus groups with the people who will use the training — CSB staff, licensed providers, faith-based organizations, peer recovery specialists.

  • What session lengths are realistic?
  • What topics are most urgent for your practice?
  • What online engagement strategies work best for you and your team? (e.g., workbooks, scenarios, reflections)

DBHDS Subject Matter Expert Workgroup Convenings

Facilitated workgroups with DBHDS subject matter experts across prevention, treatment, and recovery.

  • What does the workforce need to know?
  • What's the right sequence for learning this content?
  • What are the critical competencies for each audience?
  • What depth is appropriate for each topic?

DBHDS Existing Content Audit

Part 1: Training Materials Inventory

Design a content inventory framework for DBHDS to catalog existing training materials — webinars, slide decks, guidance documents — and assess each item's capacity to support the topics chosen.

  • What's already developed and ready to go?
  • What exists but needs reformatting?
  • What's partially developed?
  • What needs further research or development?

Part 2: Research Compilation

Identification and compilation of evidence-based research for identified topics into an APA style (7th ed.) reference list divided by topic.

Deliverables

1Scope & Sequence with Learning Tracks

Module topics, learning objectives, and sequencing organized by provider audience

2Content Inventory & Resource Library

A structured framework for cataloging existing materials by format, topic, and capacity to support the topics chosen

Evidence-based references organized by topic in APA format (7th ed.)

Years 2 and 3 Brief Summary (not included in this statement of work)
Production Plan

Prioritized build order based on Year 1 deliverables, identifying which modules get produced first, in what sequence, and at what scope.

Module Production

Instructional design, video production with a technical vendor, and subject matter expert contracting to build the prioritized modules identified in the scope and sequence.

Learning Management System

Deployment of the training system with learning tracks and progress tracking, ready for providers statewide.

All content would be packaged in industry-standard formats (SCORM, xAPI) compatible with any standards-compliant learning management system, including the DBHDS internal system. The architecture transfers regardless of where the content lives: learning tracks, scope & sequence, and content inventory are all portable.
