Buchanan County

Edit copy in Google Docs

Buchanan County is building a community schools infrastructure across all eight of its schools. The division plans to work with the Boys and Girls Club of Central Appalachia as its anchor community partner. At the elementary-middle level, the division is establishing sensory and calming spaces and dedicated STEM labs. At the high schools, the focus is expanding access to college visits and career exploration for students in one of Virginia's most geographically isolated counties.
