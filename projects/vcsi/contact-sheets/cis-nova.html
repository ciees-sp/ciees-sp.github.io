CIS Nova

Albemarle County is partnering with Communities in Schools (CIS) of NOVA to build family engagement at Greer Elementary and Journey Middle School, two schools where participation has been historically low. At Greer, the bilingual site coordinator is filling the school's family liaison role on the school attendance team and meeting with families in community locations, using their home language to share information. At Journey Middle, the coordinator is rebuilding family programming to ensure a welcoming environment for families. During the first quarter of the year, eight community events across both schools have drawn 201 attendees.

Community partners are bringing enrichment directly into the classroom at Greer Elementary, where the site coordinator's outreach has connected the school with seven local organizations. A second-grader told the coordinator, "I can be myself and you'll listen."

"Communities in Schools has introduced a number of creative arts related activities and programming at a time when there are few outlets for the arts and creative expression. The students are responding very positively."

— Journey Middle School Leadership
