CIS Richmond

Edit copy in Google Docs

Two Richmond City schools, Henry Marsh Elementary and J.L. Francis Elementary, are partnering with Communities in Schools (CIS) of Richmond. The division has included a community schools voice in the collaborative teams that support student resources, with the work focusing on attendance and mental health. At Henry Marsh, four community events have drawn 1,135 attendees through partnerships with local churches and community organizations. At J.L. Francis, a majority LatinX school, the coordinator has helped expand translation and interpretation services for bilingual families.
