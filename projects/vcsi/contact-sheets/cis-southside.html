CIS Southside

Edit copy in Google Docs

Greensville County is partnering with Communities in Schools (CIS) of Virginia's Southside affiliate across three schools at different stages of implementation. At Greensville High School, coordinators are providing food and laundry vouchers to families experiencing basic needs insecurity and building college readiness programming supported by twenty community partnerships. E.W. Wyatt Middle School is connecting students to career and technical education, substance use prevention, and work-based learning opportunities. Greensville Elementary is in early implementation, with schoolwide events planned and a counseling services partnership in place. Across all three schools, Greensville is expanding translation and interpretation services for multilingual families, addressing a specific need in the county's LatinX community.
