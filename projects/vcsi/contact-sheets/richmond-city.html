Richmond Public Schools is launching its community schools work by building a dedicated community hub at Clark Springs Center. The hub is designed to serve families and students by providing access to supports like clothing closets and food pantries that help students arrive at school ready to learn, while working to increase compliance with school health requirements, including immunizations and physical exams.

The division has a goal of doubling family engagement, from 500 to 1,000 families per year. Currently, the implementation team is finalizing the physical space, managing procurement of supplies, completing staffing, and building out an enrichment activity schedule.
